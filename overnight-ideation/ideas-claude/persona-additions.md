# Additional personas for the harness battery

**Scope:** Voter shapes not already in the §3 battery in HARNESS-HANDOFF.md (15 personas listed). What's missing? US-centric is fine but international personas welcome too.

**Each entry must include:**
- Persona name (descriptive, e.g., "Suburban Asian-American moderate D, college-educated, climate-priority")
- Demographics
- Party ID
- Node-position sketch
- Moral circle profile (universal baseline, scoped excess)
- EPS + AES leaning
- Engagement tier
- Expected archetype top-1 + acceptable top-3
- Expected vote sequence (2008–2024)
- What this persona stress-tests that the existing 15 don't

**Categories to think about:**
- Demographic gaps (Asian-American, Native, multiracial, LGBTQ+, religious minorities)
- Ideological niches (paleocon, neocon, classical liberal, social democrat distinct from progressive)
- Generational shapes (Gen Z right-coded, millennial homeowner R, boomer D)
- International personas (UK, French, German, Indian, Brazilian — does the model generalize?)
- Cross-pressure shapes (pro-life Democrat, pro-choice Republican, anti-immigration progressive on labor grounds)
- Low-information voter shapes (only votes presidential, only votes local, only votes when angry)
